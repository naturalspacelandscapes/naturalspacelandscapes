Welcome

At Natural Space Landscapes we take a professional approach in all of our work to ensure that once it is complete you are left with a garden to be proud of. Whether you are looking for commercial landscaping for your business, or a makeover for your home, we have the equipment, materials and skills to make the dream a reality.

The Services We Offer

Garden Design

Whether you want a complete redesign of your garden, a planting scheme for an existing border or simply some advice, we can help.

Patios & Paving

Patios can be an ideal low maintenance alternative to a traditional garden lawn. A natural stone patio is great if you are looking for a place to entertain guests or just to relax and enjoy your garden.

Pergola’s

A lean to pergola creates a welcoming outdoor space right on your doorstep, similar to an awning. From garden soirees to al fresco lunches, simply step outside and enjoy. Lean to pergolas maximize your available space and create a sense of flow from your home to your garden. Think of it as a modern decorative feature as well as a useful structure; your pergola will be your new favourite spot to sit and relax. Some people also call these wall mounted pergolas.
